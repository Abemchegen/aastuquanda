# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want a friendly, harassment-free experience for everyone who contributes, reviews, or participates.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and respectful.
- Assume good intent and give constructive feedback.
- Focus on what’s best for the community.
- Respect privacy and do not share other people’s personal information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Trolling, threats, or sustained disruption.

## Scope

This applies in project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions) and in any community space where the project is represented.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, lock threads, or restrict participation when needed.
